Complete Guide: Windows OS Installation from Scratch

⚠️ SAFETY & REQUIREMENTS

  • Backup: This process will wipe your "C:" Drive. Save your photos/files elsewhere first!
  • Hardware: You need a 8GB+ USB Pendrive (it will be formatted).
  • Internet: A stable connection is needed to download the ISO (approx 5GB-6GB).

Step 1: The Toolkit (Official Links)

Don't download from third-party sites. Use these official sources:

Download Windows 11 ISO Download Rufus (Bootable Tool)

Step 2: Create the Bootable USB

  1. Open Rufus and plug in your USB drive.
  2. Click SELECT and choose your downloaded Windows ISO.
  3. Partition Scheme: Choose GPT for modern PCs (UEFI) or MBR for very old PCs.
  4. Click START. Wait until the status bar says "READY."

Step 3: Booting from the USB

Insert the USB into the target PC and turn it on. Immediately tap your Boot Menu Key:

BrandBoot Key
HPF9 or Esc
Dell / LenovoF12
ASUSF8 or Esc
Acer / MSIF11

Select your USB Flash Drive from the list and press Enter.

Step 4: The Installation Screen

  1. Select Language and click Next > Install Now.
  2. When asked for a key, click "I don't have a product key" (you can activate later).
  3. Select your Windows Edition (Home or Pro).
  4. CRITICAL: Choose "Custom: Install Windows only (advanced)".
  5. Select the "Unallocated Space" or your "Drive 0" and click Next.

💡 Pro Tip

Once installed, run Windows Update immediately to get your drivers for sound, Wi-Fi, and graphics automatically.
